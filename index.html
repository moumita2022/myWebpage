Hansaa Dance Academy is a nationally acclaimed dance studio based in Munich,Germany, in a state-of-the-art facility. Dance classes are offered to students ages 2 1/2 and up, in India Classical Forms-(Kathak,Bharatnatyam,Odissi),Jazz, Ballet, Tap, Contemporary, Tumbling, Hip Hop, Fairytale Ballet and Improv.
