SUMMER INTENSIVE 2022

Our Summer Intensive includes a comprehensive curriculum rooted in techniques of Ballet and Modern with additional classes in Contemporary, Jazz, Hip Hop, African, Dunham, Tap, & Musical Theater as well as an elite roster of Guest Artists. ​ This year we are offering virtual and in-person participation options for ages 8+. HANSAA ON POINTE INTENSIVE 2022

HANSAA On Pointe Intensive is a 3 day intensive where intermediate to advanced students ages 8-25, with at least two years of classical ballet training, are invited to study with ballet masters from across the globe. Past classes have included ballet, pointe, character, men’s technique, variations, modern, Graham, jazz and contemporary repertoire, as well as ballet history.
